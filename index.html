Understanding your
PI-RADS score.

Each PI-RADS category carries different clinical implications. Dr. Patak's structured report clarifies exactly what your score means for your treatment path.

1–2
Very low / Low
Clinically significant cancer highly unlikely. No immediate further workup typically needed.
3
Intermediate — critical score
Equivocal finding. Subspecialist review helps clarify clinical relevance.
4
High probability
Finding likely clinically significant. Structured subspecialist report supports your treating physician.
5
Very high probability
Clinically significant cancer very likely. Immediate expert clarification of extent and characteristics critical.
